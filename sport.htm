The most important feature of the Elite CX is the use of carbon in the seat. Carbon fibre has a high tensile strength with an incredibly low weight. By creating a seat from this, we are able to curve the design to shape completely around the contours of your body, ensuring a maximum connection that results in any upper body movements to be transferring to the chair itself. This evening out of the energy distribution makes the chair more comfortable and improves responsiveness and performance.
